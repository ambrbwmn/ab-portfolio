hello world

I'm Amber and this is my portfolio! I am currently located in the SF Bay Area, but I have spent most of my career in Chicago. I graduated from Michigan State University with an undergrad in Finance and began my career as a Finance Analyst at a couple large companies. After a few years I discovered that finance was not the career path I wanted to pursue, and that I was much more interested in data and the stories it could tell. I decided to go back to school to pivot my career into a more data driven role.

I earned my Masters of Business Analytics from The University of Notre Dame in 2021 and never looked back. Since then I've been working in BI and Analytics, grateful to be able to do something that I am passionate about every day.

“I like to think of data as the new soil, Get in and get your hands dirty.” — David McCandless

I still feel like im just getting started and that there's so much left to learn. I'm hoping to document my progress on this platform and showcase things I'm learning along the way. Thanks for stopping by!
